The MELINDA - Mobility Ecosystem for Low-carbon and INnovative moDal shift in the Alps – project wants to generate a better understanding of mobility habits and preferences of people's daily lives.

With this survey, we wish to collect your attitudes and give you some tips on how to deal with the environmental challenges that we all must face urgently.

This survey only requires 7-8 minutes of your time.

Legal Disclaimer

You are invited to participate in a research survey, done by the MELINDA Project, particularly run by the University of Milano-Bicocca. Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and anonymous. You will not be asked to state your name.

The collected data will be stored on secure servers located in the EU and processed in accordance to GDPR.

To better understand people mobility choices, we will ask you a few questions about your travel habits, preferences, environmental awareness and for statistical classification purposes some basic socio-demographic information (age, gender, education, occupation, postal code and household dimension). Your email address, if you will provide it to us (is not compulsory), will be stored separately from the answers and used only to communicate you project's developments. The information collected may be used by selected research personnel from project partners in strict adherence to measures protecting personal identity. The aggregated findings of the study may be published and/or presented at meetings.

If you have any further questions about this project or if you have a research-related query, you may contact Simone Caiello at simone.caiello@unimib.it..
